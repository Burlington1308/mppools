    About Us

    M.P Pools Services is a reputable and reliable pool maintenance company based in Pretoria, serving Johannesburg, Centurion, and surrounding areas. Our team of experts has years of experience in providing top-notch pool services to residential and commercial clients. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and safety.


    Our services include but are not limited to:

    • Pool Cleaning and Maintenance
    • Pool Repairs and Renovations
    • Pool Equipment Installation and Repair
    • Water Testing and Balancing
    • Emergency Pool Services

    Our Process

    At M.P Pools Services, we follow a systematic approach to ensure that every pool we service is maintained to the highest standards. Our process includes:


    1. Initial Consultation: We assess your pool's condition and discuss your specific needs and preferences.
    2. Customized Service Plan: We create a tailored service plan that addresses your pool's unique requirements.
    3. Regular Maintenance: We provide regular maintenance services to keep your pool clean, safe, and in optimal condition.
    4. Emergency Services: We offer prompt emergency services for any unexpected issues that may arise with your pool.
    5. Customer Support: Our team is always available to answer your questions and provide support whenever you need it.
