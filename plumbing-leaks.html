    Frequently Asked Questions

    How can I tell if it's a leak or evaporation?

    Try the "Bucket Test". Place a bucket of water on your first step, mark the water level inside and outside the bucket. If the pool water drops faster than the bucket water, it's a leak.

    How much water can a small leak waste?

    Even a small leak can waste thousands of liters in a single month, costing you a fortune in municipal water and chemical replacements.

    Will you have to dig up my whole yard to fix a pipe?

    Not necessarily. Thanks to electronic pinpointing, we only expose the exact area of the pipe that needs repair, saving your paving and garden.
